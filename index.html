Welcome to your godot3D HUB application!
Godot is an awesome engine! If you ask anyone using it, there is no doubt that the answer you will receive is that its community the engineâ€™s greatest power. Implementing the Vulkan renderer in Godot 4 is a solid step forward which will pave the way for 3D games in Godot. Is it enough? Unfortunately, no. Basic 3D features which are one click away in other engines, are missing. One of them is the ability to draw 3D lines, similar to the Line Renderer in Unity. Another example is having the ability to draw labels and UI in the 3D space. And no, having 2D features hacked into 3D like this, does not cut it. Godot needs real 3D features. The Godot team has their hands filled with a lot of things, so its up for us, the community, to make the change. Enter: Godot 3D Hub. G3DH is the place where the Redefine Community will create and curate addons for Godot to extend its 3D functionalities. And, the first on the list is Line3D. If you would like to contribute to the initiative, you can find more information on the G3DH page.
